# Architecture Checklist

Source: `SYSTEM_PROMPT.md` §13, §86.

- ☐ Aligns with product vision (`project-memory-bank/00-project-vision.md`)
- ☐ Increases trust
- ☐ Reduces or does not needlessly increase complexity
- ☐ Can scale (§10 long-term architecture goals)
- ☐ Understandable by another engineer
- ☐ Testable
- ☐ Monitorable
- ☐ Fails safely
- ☐ High cohesion, low coupling, single responsibility (§9)
- ☐ Reuse → Extend → Refactor → Replace order respected (§21)
- ☐ Major decision recorded as an ADR if warranted (`adr/`)
